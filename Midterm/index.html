Activities

Explore Switzerland's scenery at its heart.

Skiing the Swiss Alps

From the slopes of St. Moritz to the peaks of the Matterhorn, the Swiss Alps offer world-class terrain for every skill level. Vast landscapes of freshly fallen snow and a vast network of resorts ensure an unforgettable alpine experience.

Learn More

Hiking

Traverse the iconic Eiger North Face and the gentle meadows of Lauterbrunnen to discover the diverse beauty of the Swiss countryside. Walk through lush alpine meadows and around crystal-clear lakes, all while surrounded by the vast majesty of the Swiss Alps.

Learn More

Paragliding

Atop Swiss peaks, soar above the world and witness a truly miraculous view of the Swiss landscape. First time? No problem! Accompany yourself with an expert pilot to ensure a safe yet scenic descent. Bonus points for doing a mid-air flip on the way down!

Learn More

Scenic Rail Journeys

Get a close up of Switzerland's gorgeous landscapes whilst enjoying the comfort of a train. With routes that travel through snow-capped peaks and emerald valleys, scenic rail journeys offer a one-of-a-kind way to expaerience the beauty of Switzerland.

Learn More

Locations

Great places to begin your adventure.

Grindelwald

At the foot of the Eiger North face, Grindelwald is a popular mountain village. It's location lends itself to some of the most desireable alpine excursions into the Jungfrau region. At the top lies Jungfraujoch, the Top of Europe.

Learn More

Lucerne

At the center of Switzerland, Lucerne uniquely rests between lake and mountains. The city is known for its preserved medieval architecture, such as its iconic Chapel Bridge that was constructed in the 14th century.

Learn More

Interlaken

Resting between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz, Interlaken is a hub known for its proximity to many outdoor activities. The Jungfrau region and the Bernese Oberland are both just a short train ride away, making the town a great base for exploring Switerland's outdoor scenery.

Learn More

Zermatt

Home to the iconic Matterhorn, Zermatt is a high-altitude glacial village that is located at the heart of the Valais Alps. The village offers activites during year-round, and is one of few locations worldwide to offeryear-round skiing.

Learn More
