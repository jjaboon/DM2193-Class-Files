# Fresh Guacamole Recipe
A recipe for delicious, fresh guacamole with **only 7 ingredients**. Perfect alongside tortilla chips, tacos, rice bowls, and more.

## Ingredients
- 3 Avocados
- 2 Yellow/Red Onion
- 2 Roma Tomatoes
- 1 Jalapeno Pepper (*use 1 serrano pepper instead for more heat*)
- 1 Bunch Cilantro
- 1 Lime
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Cut open 3 avocados, remove their pits, and scoop their flesh into a bowl.
2. Finely dice onions, roma tomatoes, pepper, and cilantro, and add with avocados.
3. Squeeze lime juice and add a pinch of salt into the bowl.
4. Mix the ingredients in the bowl until you reach your desired consistency.
